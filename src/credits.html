1. In general

The animation that appears in all the case studies and at the bottom of the portfolio (the points and lines) was created using Javascript and "ChatGPT". Here's what I asked the latter: "Can you help me create background animation where some points and lines interract with each other". I then adapted the code he gave me to arrive at the current result (I modified the code in terms of the number of points, their size, their colors, their speed of movements, the background color).

As for the texts, I wrote them all myself. However, when I had doubts about the wording, I would give them to an AI (Copilot or ChatGPT) to get other formulations. Once I liked the AI's formulation, I reworked it to keep a personal touch. When I wasn't sure about the translation or the wording of the sentences in English, I called on DeepL.
